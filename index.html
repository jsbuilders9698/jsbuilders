How do I install the theme?

After purchasing, you'll receive a .zip file. In your Shopify admin, go to Online Store > Themes > Upload theme. Select the .zip file and wait for it to upload. Then click "Publish" to make it your active theme. We also provide a detailed installation guide with step-by-step instructions.

Is this a one-time payment?

Yes, this is a one-time payment of $40 USDT. There are no recurring fees or hidden charges. You'll receive all future updates to the theme at no additional cost.

Can I use this theme on multiple stores?

Each purchase is valid for one Shopify store. If you want to use the theme on multiple stores, you'll need to purchase additional licenses. Contact us for bulk pricing if you need multiple licenses.

Do I need coding knowledge to use this theme?

No coding knowledge is required. Our theme is designed to be user-friendly with an intuitive customization interface. You can easily change colors, fonts, and layouts using Shopify's theme editor. For advanced customizations, basic HTML/CSS knowledge may be helpful.

Is the theme mobile responsive?

Yes, our theme is fully responsive and optimized for all devices including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. It automatically adjusts to provide the best shopping experience regardless of screen size.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the digital nature of our product, we do not offer refunds once the theme has been downloaded. However, we provide extensive documentation and support to ensure you can successfully implement the theme on your store.
